Hello

Over the years, I have met many great people of varying professions, aspirations and ambitions, but felt no closer to discovering my own. This was until realising that I needed to stop limiting my potential options to those that I believed I was capable of at that specific point in time. The liberating realisation that I can achieve anything that I set my mind to, is what has cultivated my desire for programming. I have always thought coding and tech was neat, but attempting it never crossed my mind due to my misconception that such as thing was exclusive only to the naturally gifted computer wizards of society.

Nonetheless, after doing some self learning at home through various online courses and youtube tutorials; to gauge whether or not programming is something I see myself doing in the future. I decided to fully commit by enrolling in GA’s Web Development Immersive course. As anticipated, programming has not been an easy endeavor; but surprisingly not as intimidating as my preconceptions led me to believe. GA has given me a solid foundation to be able to kick start my career as a competent developer. The most important lesson I think I’ve learned, and one I still struggle with at times, is that an Error is not a negative thing but merely a learning tool that guides you in order to become better developer.
